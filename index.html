2nd Workshop on Computational Intelligence and Smart Cities

We are very proud to announce that the 2nd edition of the Workshop on Computational Intelligence and Smart Cities (WCISC) will be held in IEEE WCCI/IJCNN 2018 conferences. Download Call for Papers

Only submissions made in IEEE WCCI system until February 1st will appear in IJCNN proceedings!

Click here to access IJCNN submission system! In field Main Research Topic, choose WT1. Workshop on Computational Intelligence and Smart Cities (it is one of the last options, look carefully!)

Papers must follow IEEE Word/LaTeX guidelines with 6-8 pages (extra pages will be charged $100 USD, up to 10 pages total). Click for more information.

Contact workshop organizers: Vitor Nazário Coelho, Thays Aparecida de Oliveira, Igor Machado Coelho, Luiz Satoru Ochi, Helena Ramalhinho Lourenço, Andréa Cynthia Santos and Miguel Oliver.
